What is Bouldering?

Bouldering is climbing made simple.

A great workout for the body and the mind, bouldering combines problem solving and physical exercise on a 3-dimensional level, providing endless possibilities for full-body workouts and fun for all.

Route Setters create boulder “problems”, which are a combination of artificial holds attached to the walls, creating a colourful path for you to solve and summit.

With walls engineered to a specific height, our specifically designed crash-pads keep you safe, while allowing you to freely attempt even the most technical of our climbs.

For young or old, tall or small, bouldering can be a full-on replacement to your workout routine, or a social, recreational activity to enjoy with friends, family or by yourself.

Outdoor Bouldering

Outdoor climbing is an adventure that takes you beyond the gym and into the wild.

Blending physical endurance with mental acuity, outdoor climbing allows you to immerse yourself in nature while challenging your body. Each climb is a unique experience, shaped by the natural landscape, offering endless opportunities to test your strength, agility, and problem-solving skills.

Instead of artificial holds, you’ll rely on the rock’s natural features—cracks, ledges, and crevices—each requiring careful planning and precise movements. Every ascent becomes a puzzle, where you must use your mind as much as your muscles to find the best route to the top.

Safety is paramount, with gear such as ropes, harnesses, and crash pads designed to protect you as you navigate the outdoors. Whether guided by an experienced climber or exploring with friends, outdoor climbing offers a secure way to experience the thrill of scaling real rock faces.

Suitable for everyone from beginners to seasoned climbers, outdoor climbing can be a rigorous workout or a leisurely day out in nature. Whether you’re seeking a solo challenge or a fun activity with others, outdoor climbing provides a perfect blend of exercise, exploration, and adventure in the great outdoors.
